Another C# port of libnoise (http://sourceforge.net/projects/libnoise/), a general-purpose library that generates three-dimensional coherent noise. 

See http://libnoise.sourceforge.net/index.html
See http://libnoisedotnet.codeplex.com/

The original one (libnoise C++) was released under the terms of the GNU Lesser General Public License.
The C# port was released under the terms of the The MIT License (MIT).
